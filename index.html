About Metl

About Metl

Unlocking the Power of Metl

Mettle /ˈmɛtl/  noun

The ability to cope well with difficulties; strength of character.

The fund takes its name from a deliberate creative compression. "Metl" is a stylised rendering of mettle — the quality of resilience, resolve, and willingness to take on hard things.

The name is the thesis. Metl backs ventures that have the mettle to go where others won't.

Entering a new market demands more than capital. It demands conviction in the face of the unfamiliar, the patience to build without a map, and the resolve to hold a position when the path is unclear. Metl captures precisely what this fund's mandate requires — from the GPs who structured it, to the founders carrying European innovation into one of the world's most complex and rewarding markets.

01
The Fund

The fund thesis is predicated on conviction that European MedTech and HealthTech ventures have a genuine place in the Indian market, a thesis that requires mettle to back.

02
The Portfolio

The companies Metl backs are not passive recipients of capital. They are European ventures making the deliberate, demanding choice to internationalise into a market with its own regulatory logic, distribution architecture, and commercial culture.

03
The Founders

Founders who say yes to India say yes to complexity. Metl is the partner that understands it, respects it, and has the experience to help them navigate and unlock markets to drive valuation uplift.
